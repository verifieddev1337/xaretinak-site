  NETLIFY ACCOUNT — FIRST-TIME SETUP (do this once)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  1. Create a free account at netlify.com if you don't
     have one already.

  2. From your dashboard click "Add new site" →
     "Deploy manually" → drag and drop the folder
     your developer provides.

  3. Your site will be live on a Netlify preview URL
     (e.g. yoursite.netlify.app) within seconds.

  AUTOMATIC REBUILDS — KEEP CONTENT FRESH
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  Your articles (Substack) and videos (YouTube) are pulled
  in at build time. To keep them up to date the site needs
  to rebuild periodically. Enable scheduled builds:

  Site Settings → Build & deploy → Scheduled builds
  → "Enable scheduled builds" → set to every 12 hours.

  This is FREE on Netlify's free tier and means new
  articles and videos appear on the site automatically
  within 12 hours of publishing, with no action from you.

  CONTACT FORM — RECEIVE MESSAGES BY EMAIL
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  Messages sent via the contact form are captured by
  Netlify. To receive them in your inbox:

  Site Settings → Integrations → Form notifications
  → Add notification → Email notification
  → Enter your email address → Save.

  You will receive an email for every new enquiry.
  All submissions are also stored under:
  Site dashboard → Forms → "contact"
